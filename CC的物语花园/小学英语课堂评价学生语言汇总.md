小学英语课堂评价学生语言汇总
==============

    之前我们一直谈论着倾听，其实不论是倾听还是展示，都需要基于评价才能有提升，老师们在课堂上往往有常用的“口头禅”最常见的莫过于“Good!””Very good！““Excellent！”之类，但是我们有时可以丰富一下自己的评价语言，特别是在词汇量有限的情况下，如何让学生听懂我们的评价语言，同时达到通过评价修正学生错误，提升学生学习水平，是个不小的挑战。今天我们就将课堂学生评价常见语言汇总，分享给大家。
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

### 一、正面肯定类

  除了笼统地夸“好”，可以表扬得更具体，让学生知道自己哪里做得好。

1.肯定努力与进步

•Thank you. 

•Good. / Very good. / Great. / Wonderful. 

•Excellent. / Perfect. 

•Nice job. / Well done. 

•You are so clever. 

•That's perfect. 

•You did a very good job.

• Very good, boys and girls. 

•Very beautiful name, Stella. 

2.肯定发音与表达

•I love the keywords you used. 

•You have a very good memory. 

•You are so clever. 

•Wonderful, OK, thank you for your answer. 

•It's wonderful, you have good idea. 

•You think English is very easy, wonderful! 

•Your English is so great. 

•Very good, good answer. 

•Your English is so much better than many many of your friends. Louis is very good. 

3.肯定创意与思维

•Your idea is very good. 

•What a creative idea!

•I never thought of that!

•That's a very unique point.

•You have a great imagination.

•Your answer surprised me (in a good way)!

•Very good, you deserve the second gold medal. 

4\. 肯定书写与习惯

•Give me five. 

•Big hands for her/him. 

•Good try. 

•Thank you for listening. And you can be a good friend. 

•You are wonderful. 

•Wow! Very good job. 

•I love your handwriting. 

•Super cool! 

•Good to know you all. 

•Good job, very good, good memories. 

### 二、鼓励提升类

当学生不会、说错或胆小时，营造安全感至关重要。

1\. 鼓励再试一次

•Have another go. / Give it another shot.

•Take your time, I’ll wait for you.

•Almost there, try once more.

•One more time, please.

•Try again. 

•Say it again. 

2\. 引导正确回答 
-----------

•It starts with the letter 'B'.

•You're very close.

•Think about what we learned yesterday.

•The first part is correct, think about the ending.

•Say it with me. / Let's say it together.

•Louder, please. /Speak a little bit louder. 

•Next time try to speak a little bit louder so everybody can hear you. 

•Don't be nervous. 

•You can get it. 

•That's normal, you can do it. 

### 三、引导启发类

增加一些激发高阶思维和同伴互助的用语。

1\. 激发好奇心与思考
------------

•What happens next? (

•Why do you think so? (

•Can you explain it in your own words?

•How do you know that?

•Can you give me an example? ()

•What do you think will happen? (

2\. 鼓励同伴互动
----------

•Ask your partner.

•Help each other out.

•Tell your friend what you think.

•Does everyone agree? 

•Can you add to his/her idea? (

3\. 给出具体任务建议
------------

•Circle the key words. ()

•Underline the sentence. ()

•Use the picture to help you. (

•Check your own work first. (

•Listen carefully to the audio.

•Any other reasons?

•Who can try? 

•Who would like to try? 

•Any different ideas? 

•Any other wants to share? 

•Think about it. 

•Discuss with your partner. 

•Work in groups of four. 

•Let's do it together. 

•Follow me. 

•Read after me. 

在日常课堂中按这三类去使用，课堂评价体系就立体多了：正面肯定给成就感，鼓励提升建安全感，引导启发促思考。希望对大家有帮助。
